I primarily focus on capturing sincere moments in my work - catching candid, authentic scenes and expressions. I am not keen on heavy post production, I prefer a subtle approach that ever so slightly enhances the reality.

Looking back at my photographs, I see a trend: shots taken spontaneously, or by surprise are usually the most beautiful ones.

Relationship with nature is very important to me and this is translated into my work - it gives me a lot of satisfaction when I can take photographs of people immersed in the beauty of nature.

What I love most about photography is that it opens our eyes to things that usually go unnoticed and it creates memories of those most special times of our lives.

About me

Hi, I'm Ewelina!

I am a Polish born, London based photographer. Obsessed with the golden hour- chances are if there is an amazing sunset on the horizon, I am standing with my camera taking photos of it!

I love photography in all its aspects. I am most excited shooting portraits, nature and architecture. I have worked on numerous projects including personal branding, fashion, art, real estate and interior design.

My clients consists of couples, families, small business owners, artists, painters, musicians, and so on.

I try to spend as much time outdoors as possible, I love travelling and exploring new places, I never forget to take my camera with me - I need to be covered in case I notice something worth capturing :)

When the weather is less kind, I love spending time cuddling with my other half watching movies & our favourite tv series.

In case you are wondering how my adventure with photography started.

I have always loved taking photographs & having my photos taken too, but back in 2016 I went on a trip to Iceland with a specific goal of seeing the Northern Lights.

This is when I had to learn all about the manual shooting mode (if you do not know how to operate it, you CANNOT capture the light festival on the night sky!). I immediately fell in love with the process - the technical aspects combined with creative flair were pretty challenging but equally satisfying!
